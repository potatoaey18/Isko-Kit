Enrollment Guide

Step-by-step enrollment assistance

Pre-Enrollment Checklist
  • Check your academic standing and clearances
  • Consult with your academic adviser
  • Prepare required documents (Form 137, certificates, etc.)
  • Review the curriculum and plan your subjects
  • Check scholarship eligibility and requirements
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection for online enrollment
Enrollment Process
  • Access the PUP Student Information System (SIS)
  • Log in using your student credentials
  • Select subjects according to your curriculum
  • Check for schedule conflicts and room availability
  • Submit your enrollment form for validation
  • Wait for approval from the registrar's office
  • Print your Certificate of Registration (COR)
  • Process payment for tuition and fees
Required Documents
  • Completely filled enrollment form
  • Certificate of Registration (COR)
  • Official receipt of payment
  • Copy of grades from previous semester
  • Medical certificate (if required)
  • Scholarship forms
Important Reminders
  • Enroll within the designated enrollment period
  • Late enrollment may incur additional fees
  • Keep all enrollment document copies
  • Immediately report any discrepancies
  • Regularly check your official schedule
  • Attend first class day to avoid being dropped
